Our Story

Founded by Karel Daems in the 1980s and taken over by his son Jan Daems in 2007, Daems Racing has spent nearly four decades at the sharp end of European karting.

A specialist in Rotax engines and Tony Kart chassis, Jan raced alongside names like Jan Heylen and Kimi Räikkönen before building the team into one of the most respected names in the paddock.
